# 📊 Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end Power BI Business Intelligence project built using the Brazilian Olist E-Commerce dataset. This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights across sales, customers, products, sellers, and logistics.

## 🚀 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce performance and provide stakeholders with a comprehensive view of business operations through interactive dashboards.

The solution includes multiple analytical pages covering:

- Executive Overview
- Customer Analytics
- Logistics & Delivery Analytics
- Seller Analytics
- Product Analytics

The dashboard enables users to monitor KPIs, identify trends, evaluate operational efficiency, and uncover growth opportunities.

---

## 📌 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue and order performance
- Understand customer distribution and purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Evaluate seller performance and regional contribution
- Monitor logistics efficiency and delivery performance
- Explore the relationship between delivery time and customer satisfaction

---

# 📈 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of business performance.

### KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Review Score
- Average Delivery Days

### Visuals
- Revenue Trend
- Top Product Categories
- Payment Mode Preference
- Order Status Overview
- Seller Revenue Analysis

### Key Insights
- Revenue exceeded $16M
- Average delivery time is 12.5 days
- Credit Card is the dominant payment method
- Delivered orders account for over 97% of total orders

---

## 2️⃣ Customer Analytics Dashboard

Provides insights into customer behavior and geographic distribution.

### KPIs
- Total Customers
- Revenue per Customer
- Average Order Value
- Total States Served
- Review Score

### Visuals
- Customer Distribution by City
- Customer Revenue Analysis
- Customer Satisfaction vs Delivery Time
- Geographic Customer Insights

### Key Insights
- São Paulo is the largest customer market
- Customer satisfaction decreases as delivery time increases
- Customer concentration is heavily skewed toward major urban centers

---

## 3️⃣ Logistics & Delivery Dashboard

Analyzes order fulfillment and delivery performance.

### KPIs
- Average Delivery Days
- On-Time Delivery %
- Total Freight Cost
- Fastest Delivery Hours
- Review Score

### Visuals
- Delivery Time Trend
- Slowest Delivery States
- Order Status Distribution
- Customer Satisfaction vs Delivery Time

### Key Insights
- On-Time Delivery Rate exceeds 92%
- Northern states experience longer delivery times
- Faster deliveries correlate with higher review scores

---

## 4️⃣ Seller Analytics Dashboard

Evaluates seller contribution and marketplace performance.

### KPIs
- Total Sellers
- Revenue per Seller
- Total Seller Cities
- On-Time Orders

### Visuals
- Top Sellers by Revenue
- Revenue by Seller State
- Seller State Performance Table

### Key Insights
- São Paulo dominates seller revenue contribution
- Revenue generation is concentrated among a small group of sellers
- Seller distribution spans over 600 cities

---

## 5️⃣ Product Analytics Dashboard

Analyzes product performance, category contribution, and pricing trends.

### KPIs
- Total Products
- Total Categories
- Revenue per Product
- Average Product Price

### Visuals
- Top Product Categories
- Orders by Product Category
- Average Price by Category
- Product Category Revenue Analysis

### Key Insights
- Health & Beauty is the highest revenue-generating category
- Bed Bath Table leads in order volume
- Product pricing varies significantly across categories
